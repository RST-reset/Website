_Frequently Asked Questions

_What is the duration of the program?

The program duration varies depending on the specific program and partner you choose. Typically, programs are 3,6 or 12 months

_How does this [RST] work?

We work by adding an experience to existing exchange programs from our partners? We analyze you while you analyze yourself so we can make the best possible informed report about who you are and what you could do next.

_What should I expect from this [RST]?

You will be expected once a month to complete a chalanging test. Sometimes this happens alone and sometimes it happens in group.

_How much does [RST] cost?

[RST] has a fixed cost of 20 euro a month. The cost of the exchange programs itself varies depending on the specific program and the location you will be traveling to. Please take a look at our partners website for a detailed cost.

_How do I register for [RST]?

Registering for [RST] will be possible after registering for an exchange program from one of our partners. More information will follow in your letterbox.
